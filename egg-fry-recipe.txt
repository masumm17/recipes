Ingredients:
 - 1 egg
 - 1 tablespoon oil
 - 1/4 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 teaspoon onion slice
 - 1 piece green chilli slice

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking
    pans.
 
 2. Break the egg and pour it in a small bowl adn mix the sliced onion, green chilli, salt.
 
 3. Pour oil on the fry pan and wait to hit the oil.

 4. Pour the mixture and bake it on a medium hit.
